In this unit, I learned about the basics of web development, including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Our main project in this unit is creating a website portfolio to showcase our learning.

Check out my practice websites at the bottom of the page or click the screenshot to view my class 2 website.

Design Choices

The main design choices I made for my portforlio website is the dark sci-fiy color scheme and the cool animated navigation bar.

To accomplish the neonish color scheme, I changed the link color with CSS, and choose very contrasty colors that feel un-realistic.

I also put a lot of effort in to the navigation bar at the top, making a subtle animation of it's purple background color to grab user attention.

Lastly I also used this cool Audiowide font downloaded from google fonts.
