PB&J Pseudocode

  1. Open the bag of bread from the opening end
  2. Take out two pieces of bread from the bag and put it on the plate
  3. Put the bag to the side
  4. Put the two pieces of bread separated on the plate
  5. Take the jelly can and twist lid until it opens
  6. Put the lid on the side
  7. hold the non-sharp end of the knife
  8. Stick the sharp end of the knife in to the opening of the jelly can
  9. Gather a spoonful size of jelly on the blade side of the knife
  10. >Move the knife on to one of the bread, while balancing the jelly on the knife
  11. Tilt the knife so the jam drops on the bread
  12. Spread the jam evenly with the knife so 80% of the bread is covered with jelly
  13. Lay knife down beside the plate
  14. >Take the jelly can lid
  15. Match lid with jelly can opening
  16. Twist until close
  17. Take a tissue
  18. Grab the knife using the non-sharp end
  19. Match sharp end of the knife to the tissue
  20. Wipe the knife by rubbing both side of the sharp end of the knife using the wipe
  21. After the knife is clean without jelly set knife and tissue separately aside
  22. Take the butter can and twist lid until it opens
  23. Put the lid on the side
  24. hold the non-sharp end of the knife
  25. Stick the sharp end of the knife in to the opening of the pbutter can
  26. Gather a spoonful size of pbutter on the blade side of the knife
  27. Move the knife on to the bread without jelly, while balancing the butter on the knife
  28. Tilt the knife so the pbutter drops on the bread
  29. >Spread the butter evenly with the knife so 80% of the bread is covered with butter
  30. Lay knife down beside the plate
  31. Take the butter can lid
  32. Match lid with butter can opening
  33. Twist until close
  34. Take the butter spread bread
  35. Put it on top of the jelly spread bread
