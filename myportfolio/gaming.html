Duck BBreaker — Final p5.js Game

My original culminating game. Control the duck with your mouse, launch the ring upward, and smash every brick before the ring falls past you. Custom sprites made in Adobe Illustrator, original sound effect, and three speed settings.

How to Play: Move your mouse left and right to slide the duck. The ring launches automatically. Clear all the bricks to advance levels. You have 3 lives — don't let the ring fall below the duck! Choose your difficulty on the title screen: Slow / Medium / Fast.

Platformer — Game Tutorial (p5.js)

The in-class platformer tutorial that taught me gravity, collision detection, and coin collection. Platforms and coins are randomly generated each level — collect all 8 coins to advance!

How to Play: Use the ← → arrow keys to move and ↑ to jump. Collect all the gold coins to spawn a new level. If you fall off the screen you respawn at the top. See how high you can get your score!

Clicker Game — Milestone 2 (p5.js)

A 30-second reflex challenge built as Unit 4 Milestone 2. Click the moving circle as many times as you can before the timer hits zero.

How to Play: Click the blue circle — it jumps to a new random spot each time you hit it. Score as many clicks as possible in 30 seconds. Click anywhere on the game-over screen to restart and try to beat your score!
